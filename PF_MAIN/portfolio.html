Anomaly Detection from Videos

I did my master's research at CIM, McGill University in the Intelligent Multimodal Video Analysis lab. During this time, I was able to propose a novel algorithm for generalized anomaly detection from videos using convolutional autoencoders by adaptive clustering in the latent space. A fully customizable, variable-length, open-source synthetic dataset generator was developed along with this work.
